Welcome To The Bob's Burgers Memory Game!

Rules.

Flip the burger over to reveal the character.

Match two of the front facing burgers to there identical burger.

Your time starts, on the first flip & you are counted on the number of burger combos it takes to complete the game.

You can only flip two burgers a time. If they dont match they flip back!

To complete the game you need to match all burgers with their front face.
